ABOUT

I'm Adam, a hands-on product design leader with 15+ years experience building teams and shipping products for organisations including The Economist, BBC, Finnair, Expedia, Skype and Cannes Lions.

Over that time I've grown from craft into leadership. I've scaled globally distributed teams, staying close to the work that ships and generated impact in revenue, retention, and engagement.

At The Economist, I established the product design function growing from 8 to 16 people, redesigned our mobile app delivering increased engagement, launched a new 0-1 premium video proposition, overhauled subscription journeys with material uplift in conversion and customer lifetime value and integrated consumer facing AI features.

A decade at strategic design consultancy Made by Many taught me the discipline of designing through ambiguous, high-stakes problems and leading a team of 7 designers to deliver meaningful outcomes. Clients included ITV, Skype, Carlsberg, Cannes Lions, Gates Foundation, Finnair, Expedia and World Economic Forum.
